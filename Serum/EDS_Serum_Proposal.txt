EDS SERUM PROTOCOL — PROJECT OVERVIEW
-------------------------------------
• Proposed by: LeOrbis
• For Personalized EDS IV Therapy

This packet contains a plan for innovative, integrated therapy for Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome. It is based on a synthesis of current research, clinical guidelines, and personal experience with malabsorption and metabolic issues. My aim is to work with a collaborative provider to monitor, adjust, and safely optimize this approach while keeping robust logs and safety tracking.
