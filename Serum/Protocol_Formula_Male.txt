**Cycle Plan:**
- **Loading/Repletion Phase:** Start at “Start Dose,” increase to “Target Dose” over 1–2 months.
- **Therapeutic Phase:** Maintain “Target Dose” for 4–6 months.
- **Taper Phase:** Reduce dose gradually to maintenance (based on labs/symptoms/improvements).
- **Monitor for accumulation from diet or other sources—adjust as needed, especially for zinc, copper, iron, D, magnesium, and calcium.**

**Diet Coordination Plan:**
- High protein, high fat (meats, nuts, fruits); low green leafy veg for histamine/copper/zinc control.
- Serum as base layer, diet as flexible support—track both routinely.

**Monitoring:**
- Weekly symptom tracking (energy, pain, muscle/joint, mood, GI, sleep, skin, bruising)
- Monthly labs: CBC, iron/ferritin, B12, D3, Ca, Mg, K, Zn/Cu, testosterone, estradiol, Hct.

**Countermeasures:**  
As per serum protocol table and clinical best practices
