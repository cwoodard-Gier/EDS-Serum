---------------------------------------------------------------
NOTES:
-------
- Estrogen supplementation is **not routine** and may worsen hypermobility/flaring—use only for menopause, ovarian insufficiency, etc., and titrate carefully.
- Consider DHEA or microdose testosterone for severe tissue fragility, but monitor for side effects and discontinue if masculinization or mood issues arise.
- Track menstrual symptoms, period regularity, and any changes in pain, mobility, or bruising.
- If patients are cycling, adjust doses/track per cycle phase for maximum safety.
- All doses should be titrated per lab monitoring, clinical response, and patient preference.

---------------------------------------------------------------
MONITORING PLAN
---------------
- Weekly symptom log (with cycle phase): energy, pain, GI, mood, joint stability, skin healing, bruising, menses
- Monthly labs: CBC, iron/ferritin, B12, D3, Ca, Mg, K, Zn/Cu, DHEA, E2, T, Hct, bone density (as needed)
- Track food/supplemental intake, dose timing, and any cycle-related flares
